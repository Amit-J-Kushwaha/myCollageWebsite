Examination

General Structure

  • Internal

    • Under CBCS pattern, internal evaluation weightage is of 30 marks for all semester
      Internal assessment of students will be carried out as per the following module:

      1. Paper wise Minimum 1 Term End exam of 10 marks weightage, duration: 1 hour

      20 marks paper, 4 questions of 5 marks each with internal option - one from each unit, final score to be divided by 2.

    • 2. Assignment: 10 Marks

      2 long questions per unit to be submitted.

    • 3. MCQ Test: 5 Marks

      The MCQ test to be administered along with the term end exam and an additional 15 minutes will be given.

    • 4. Attendance: 5 Marks

      As per the University rule 80% attendance is must.

  • Under CBCS & semester system the Final Examination is conducted by the Sardar Patel University, Vallabh Vidyanagar (For Sy and Ty students) and by the CVM University, Vallabh Vidyanagar (For Fy Students) at the end of respective semester.

    Assignment 10 + MCQ 5 + Term Ending Exam 10 + Attendance 5 marks = 30
