Who We Are ??

V.P.&R.P.T.P. Science College, one of Gujarat's oldest, established in 1947, is managed by Charutar Vidya Mandal and affiliated with Sardar Patel University.
Initially an Arts and Science College, it fully shifted to Science in 1959. Awarded Best Science College in Gujarat thrice,
it hosted notable figures like Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, Dr. C.V. Raman, and Shri Prithviraj Kapoor. The campus, spanning 6412.45 sq. meters,
features well-equipped facilities, a library, computer center, and a central hall for 1500 students.
With 10 departments and 42 teaching faculty members, the college emphasizes science education.

Mission: We are committed to uplifting rural youth through scholastic excellence and value education, aiming to cultivate globally competitive citizens who actively contribute to nation-building.



Our vision is to educate, empower, and prepare a globally competitive human resource by fostering an environment conducive to learning, research, and critical thinking in the sciences. This will lead to the creation of a robust institution with a strong connection to its alumni.



Our goals include creating a vibrant learning environment, providing globally relevant education rooted in eternal human values, recognizing and rewarding excellence in both academic and extra-curricular pursuits, nurturing innovative entrepreneurial abilities, and enhancing lifelong socio-economic value-addition.



We hold dear the values of Equity, Accountability, Diversity, Excellence, Respect, and Compassion, which guide our actions and decisions towards a brighter future.
