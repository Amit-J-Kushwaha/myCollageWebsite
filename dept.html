About computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department was established during the beginning of the computer revolution in 1986 and the first batch had 15 students. Since then the dept has gone from strength to strength and the student intake was increased to 60 to cater to the high demand.


The department has a an advanced laboratory with a total of 46 PCs of the latest configuration. The lab is also equipped with Printer, LCD Projector, Scanner and other peripherals. The department has 20 different Licensed Software.


We teach one course at foundation level in the first and second semester and as allied and main courses from Semester III to Semester VI.


The Computer Science dept is also involved in the providing Internet facility to students via a separate Laboratory with suitable facility as well as through WI-FI facility. The dept also helps in the maintenance of all computers in the college The dept regularly conducts Add-on certificate course on ”Software Development”, one day Inter College Programming / Web Designing Competition “Compu-Carnival” for S. P. Uni. Students, Industrial Visits and Expert Talk every year.


The department has developed various software and programs like for administrative office admission work, Payroll, Marksheet, Attendance Management System, etc

The Department of Mathematics was established in 1947. At present there are 9 faculty members in the department.


Right from the beginning its faculty members have taught in many pure as well as applied branches of
mathematics like Real and Complex Analysis, Topology, Metric Spaces, Algebra, Numerical Analysis, Number Theory, Classical Mechanics, Graph Theory, Calculus and Differential Equations.
The department started manual as well as software based Mathematics practical for the first time in Gujarat.
The Department is keen at identifying current trends in Mathematics education and organizes workshops for mathematics community at regular intervals.
The department also provides an opportunity to all the students of the college to learn LaTex , a computer language for learning mathematical and technical typesetting through an Add-On course.

The college, managed by Charutar Vidya Mandal (CVM) introduced the non- traditional B.Sc. Industrial Chemistry course in June 1973.
The graduates with industrial chemistry are better suited for the job requirements in the industrial environment. As the course covers almost all aspects of chemical industry, these students are suitable for any department in chemical industry like production, quality control, product development etc. The syllabi have been framed to cater the needs of chemical industries. Hence the faculties comprise teachers with qualifications from various branches viz. pure chemistry, chemical technology, chemical engineering, and polymer and petroleum technology. The department maintains consistence rapport with nearby industries of relevant specialization with a view to produce readily acceptable candidates to cater to their manpower requirements. On research front the faculty members have together more than 100 publications in various journals of national and international repute. The department is also recognized for registering students for Ph.D. in industrial chemistry.

Established in tandem with the computer revolution in 1986, our BCA Department at VP & RPTP Science College has seen remarkable growth. Initially accommodating 15 students, the department now thrives with an intake of 60, meeting high demand.


Our advanced laboratory, equipped with 46 cutting-edge PCs, a printer, LCD projector, scanner, and 20 licensed software, supports a dynamic learning environment. We offer foundational courses in the first two semesters and specialized courses from Semester III to VI.

Similar to the Computer Science Department, we've developed software for administrative tasks, admissions, payroll, marksheets, and an Attendance Management System. Our commitment to staying at the forefront of technology ensures a comprehensive and enriching educational experience for our BCA students.
