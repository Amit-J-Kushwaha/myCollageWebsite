Established with historical significance, this venerable institution in Gujarat was inaugurated by
the esteemed "Iron Man of India," Sardar Patel, in 1947.
Pioneering academic excellence, it proudly secured an "A" grade during its NAAC Re-Accreditation in both 2013-18 and its ongoing third cycle (2020-2025),
reflecting a commendable commitment to quality education.

Recognized by the Government of Gujarat as the best science college thrice, it stands as a beacon of educational distinction.
Achieving a coveted CPE-II status and boasting a Wi-Fi campus, the college combines tradition with modernity.
Notably, it clinched the 61st position in India according to the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2017,
showcasing its national standing.

Accolades continue with a Four Star Rating by the Gujarat State Institution Ranking Framework (GSIRF),
reaffirming its regional prominence. Additionally,
the institution's commitment to environmental sustainability is evident through its "A+" re-accreditation by
AAA (KCG) and an "A" rating from the Core Committee of Green Audit Cell for exemplary Green Audit Activities.

In essence, this college weaves a tapestry of heritage, academic excellence,
and technological advancement, epitomizing a holistic and progressive approach to education.
